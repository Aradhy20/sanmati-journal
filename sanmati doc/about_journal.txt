“Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse” is a national-level multidisciplinary quarterly research journal established in 2026 with the objective of promoting high-quality academic research, intellectual dialogue, and innovative scholarship across diverse disciplines. Published in print (offline) format with ISSN (Print): 3108-1819, the journal serves as a credible and structured platform for scholars, academicians, researchers, and practitioners to disseminate original research and contribute to the evolving landscape of knowledge. The journal is bilingual in nature, accepting submissions in both English and Hindi, thereby ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for a wide academic audience. In the contemporary global knowledge ecosystem, where disciplinary boundaries are increasingly fluid, the journal emphasizes multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary approaches that integrate insights from arts, humanities, social sciences, media studies, and scientific domains.





The primary vision of “Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse” is to emerge as a leading academic platform at both national and international levels, recognized for its scholarly rigor, ethical standards, and meaningful contributions to knowledge advancement. The journal aims to bridge traditional knowledge systems with contemporary research paradigms while fostering a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and inclusiveness. Its mission is to encourage original and impactful research, provide opportunities for early-career researchers and young scholars, and promote collaborative and interdisciplinary academic engagement. By supporting the dissemination of research that is socially relevant and policy-oriented, the journal contributes to the broader goal of societal development and intellectual progress.





The scope of the journal is broad and inclusive, covering a wide range of disciplines including arts and humanities, social sciences, media and communication studies, natural and applied sciences, and emerging interdisciplinary fields. It welcomes a variety of scholarly contributions such as research articles, review papers, case studies, thematic essays, book reviews, and papers presented at conferences and seminars. 

Particular emphasis

 is placed on research that addresses contemporary challenges and provides practical insights, thereby ensuring that the published work is not only theoretically sound but also socially and academically relevant. This inclusive scope enables the journal to act as a dynamic forum for the exchange of ideas and perspectives across disciplines.





The editorial philosophy of the journal is grounded in academic excellence, transparency, and inclusiveness. The editorial board is committed to maintaining high standards of scholarly quality by ensuring that all submissions undergo a rigorous and fair evaluation process. The journal encourages diverse perspectives and seeks to create an intellectual environment where innovative ideas, critical debates, and interdisciplinary discussions can flourish. It also recognizes the importance of nurturing emerging scholars by offering constructive feedback and guidance, thereby contributing to the development of a robust and sustainable research culture.





To ensure the integrity and quality of published research, the journal follows a strict double-blind peer review system, in which the identities of both authors and reviewers are kept confidential. This process eliminates bias and ensures an objective evaluation of each manuscript. Submissions are assessed by subject experts based on originality, relevance, methodological rigor, clarity of presentation, and contribution to existing knowledge. Only those manuscripts that meet the journal’s academic standards are accepted for publication, thereby maintaining the credibility and scholarly value of the journal.





The journal also upholds a stringent policy against plagiarism and is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity. All manuscripts are screened using plagiarism detection tools, and only those that comply with the similar index guidelines prescribed by regulatory authorities such as UGC are considered for publication. Any form of academic misconduct, including plagiarism, data fabrication, or duplicate submission, results in immediate rejection. This strict adherence to ethical standards ensures the authenticity and reliability of the research published in the journal.





The submission process is designed to be systematic and user-friendly. Authors are required to submit their manuscripts in MS Word format (.doc/.docx), using Times New Roman font for English and Mangal Unicode for Hindi. The formatting guidelines specify a 14-point bold title, 12-point regular text, and 1.5-line spacing. Each manuscript must include an abstract of 150–250 words along with 4–6 keywords. The number of co-authors is limited to a maximum of two, and references must be formatted according to APA style. Submissions are accepted via email at sanmatijournal@gmail.com, along with complete author details, institutional affiliation, and contact information.





A key strength of “Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse” lies in its focus on emerging research areas that are of contemporary academic and societal importance. These include fields such as artificial intelligence and society, digital humanities, climate change and sustainability, media and democracy, gender studies, social justice, globalization, and Indian knowledge systems. By encouraging research in these domains, the journal remains relevant to current global challenges while fostering innovation and forward-thinking scholarship.





The journal also places a strong emphasis on the Indian context while maintaining global relevance. It encourages research that explores the rich diversity of Indian society, culture, education, and intellectual traditions, while simultaneously engaging with global theories and perspectives. This dual focus enables the journal to contribute meaningfully to both national and international academic discourse, creating a balanced and holistic approach to knowledge production.





Looking toward the future, the journal aspires to achieve international recognition by being indexed into reputed databases such as Scopus, Web of Science, and ABDC. Plans are underway to expand into digital platforms, establish collaborations with international institutions, and adopt advanced publishing standards such as DOI and Crossruff integration. These initiatives aim to enhance the journal’s visibility, accessibility, and academic impact on a global scale while maintaining commitment to quality and ethical publishing practices.





In essence, “Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse” is not merely a publication 

platform

 but a dynamic academic initiative dedicated to fostering intellectual growth, interdisciplinary collaboration, and ethical research practices. It serves as a bridge between knowledge creation and its practical application in society, benefiting academicians, researchers, policymakers, and students alike. With its commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and innovation, the journal is poised to become a respected and influential platform in the academic community, contributing significantly to the advancement of knowledge and the development of a progressive and informed society.
