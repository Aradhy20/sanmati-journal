Publication &amp; Policy A- Publication Ethics Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse is committed to the highest standards of academic quality, research integrity, and ethical publishing. The journal follows COPE guidelines and expects authors, editors, and reviewers to uphold fairness, transparency, and responsibility. Any form of research misconduct is examined and addressed appropriately. B- Peer Review Policy All submitted manuscripts undergo a Double-Blind Peer Review process, in which the identities of both authors and reviewers are kept confidential. This process ensures fairness, objectivity, originality, and academic rigor. Manuscripts are evaluated based on their scholarly merit, relevance, clarity, and contribution to knowledge. C- Plagiarism Policy The journal follows a zero-tolerance policy toward plagiarism. All submissions are screened for originality. Manuscripts with a Similarity Index exceeding 20% (excluding references) are rejected without review. 5. Submission Process Manuscripts are submitted via email to sanmatijournal@gmail.com . Initial screening is conducted for scope, format, and plagiarism. Editorial Screening The Editor-in-Chief and Editors assess manuscripts for relevance, originality, and academic quality. Manuscripts not meeting basic criteria may be rejected at this stage. Peer Review Process Suitable manuscripts undergo Double-Blind Peer Review , ensuring anonymity of authors and reviewers. Reviewers evaluate originality, methodology, clarity, relevance, and contribution to knowledge. Review comments and revision suggestions are shared with authors, where applicable. Editorial Decision Based on reviewers’ reports, manuscripts may be: Accepted Accepted with minor revisions Accepted with major revisions Rejected Revision &amp; Acceptance Revised manuscripts must be submitted within the stipulated timeframe. Final acceptance is granted after satisfactory compliance with reviewer comments. Publication Accepted manuscripts are scheduled for publication in the forthcoming issue. Authors are informed of publication details via email. The journal ensures fairness, confidentiality, and academic integrity at every stage of the editorial and peer review process. 6. Compliance A- Copyright Policy Authors publishing in Sanmati Spectrum of Knowledge &amp; Emerging Discourse retain the copyright of their work. By submitting their manuscript, authors grant the journal the right of first publication. Proper acknowledgment of all sources is mandatory. B- Open Access Policy The journal follows a full Open Access policy, allowing readers to read, download, copy, share, or link to articles, provided the original work is properly cited. All published content is archived digitally and in print for long-term academic preservation and accessibility. C- Author Declaration All authors must submit a signed Author Declaration Form along with their manuscript. Authors confirm that: The manuscript is original and has not been published or submitted elsewhere. All sources are properly cited and acknowledged. There is no conflict of interest. The manuscript has been checked for plagiarism, and the Similarity Index is below 20%. Signed declarations must be submitted via email: sanmatijournal@gmail.com Manuscripts submitted without the Author Declaration Form will not be reviewed.
